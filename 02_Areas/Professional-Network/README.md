## Overview & Principles

Building and maintaining a strategic professional network focused on AI product management, technology leadership, and cross-industry connections. Emphasizes authentic relationship building, value creation, and long-term mutual benefit.

## Current Focus Areas

### Q1 2025 Goals
- [ ] Build network of 50+ AI/PM professionals across target companies
- [ ] Conduct 20+ informational interviews with industry leaders
- [ ] Establish thought leadership through content sharing and discussions
- [ ] Participate in 10+ industry events (virtual and in-person)

### Key Responsibilities
- **Relationship Building** - Cultivating meaningful professional connections
- **Industry Engagement** - Active participation in AI/PM communities
- **Value Creation** - Sharing insights, making introductions, offering help
- **Reputation Management** - Building personal brand and professional credibility

## Success Metrics

- **Network Growth** - Quality connections in target industries/roles
- **Engagement Level** - Regular meaningful interactions with network
- **Value Exchange** - Helping others and receiving support
- **Opportunities Generated** - Referrals, insights, collaboration offers

## Maintenance Practices

### Weekly
- Reach out to 3-5 new potential connections
- Engage with existing network on LinkedIn/social platforms
- Share valuable content or insights with community
- Follow up on recent conversations and introductions

### Monthly
- Conduct 3-5 informational interviews
- Attend 2+ networking events or industry meetups
- Review and organize contact database
- Send value-add messages to key connections

### Quarterly
- Deep relationship audit and prioritization
- Major networking event participation (conferences)
- Thought leadership content creation
- Network strategy refinement based on career goals

## Key Principles

1. **Give Before You Get** - Lead with value and helpfulness
2. **Quality Over Quantity** - Deep relationships beat superficial connections
3. **Authentic Engagement** - Genuine interest in others' work and challenges
4. **Long-term Perspective** - Build relationships beyond immediate job needs

## Network Strategy for Career Transition

1. **Target Companies** - Connect with people at desired organizations
2. **Role Models** - Build relationships with successful AI PMs
3. **Cross-functional** - Include engineers, designers, executives
4. **Industry Diversity** - AI across different sectors and applications

## Connection Categories

### Primary Targets
- **AI Product Managers** - Direct role models and peers
- **Engineering Leaders** - Technical partnership and credibility
- **Executive Leadership** - Decision makers and strategic thinkers

### Supporting Network
- **Career Changers** - Others transitioning into AI/PM
- **Mentors** - Experienced professionals willing to guide
- **Industry Experts** - Thought leaders and subject matter experts

## Related Areas

- **Career-Development** - Network supports job search and growth
- **Health** - Social connections contribute to well-being
- **Finance** - Network may provide salary insights and opportunities
