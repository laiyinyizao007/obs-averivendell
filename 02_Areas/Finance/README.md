- **Budget Accuracy** - Monthly variance < 5% from plan
- **Emergency Fund** - 6+ months expenses saved
- **Salary Intelligence** - Current market data for target roles
- **Investment Performance** - Meeting long-term growth targets

## Maintenance Practices

### Weekly
- Expense tracking and categorization
- Bill payments and account monitoring
- Investment portfolio review
- Financial goal progress check

### Monthly
- Detailed budget review and adjustment
- Net worth calculation and tracking
- Career salary research updates
- Investment rebalancing assessment

### Quarterly
- Comprehensive financial health review
- Tax preparation and optimization
- Insurance coverage evaluation
- Long-term goal adjustment

## Key Principles

1. **Transparency** - Track everything, understand all financial flows
2. **Strategic Thinking** - Align spending with career and life goals
3. **Risk Management** - Maintain appropriate emergency reserves
4. **Informed Decisions** - Research thoroughly before major financial choices

## Financial Priorities During Career Transition

1. **Maintain Stability** - Sufficient reserves for job search period
2. **Invest in Growth** - Education, networking, professional development
3. **Negotiate Wisely** - Research market rates thoroughly
4. **Plan Long-term** - Consider total compensation, not just base salary
